Native Integrations

Google Calendar

A Snap button appears directly within the event. Click it and Harvest opens pre-filled with the event name — no typing, no tab switching.

  • Button embedded directly within the event
  • Event title auto-filled in Harvest
  • Works for scheduled events and appointments

Notion

A Snap button appears in the Notion toolbar next to Share. Click it and Harvest opens pre-filled with the current page title.

  • Button sits natively in the Notion toolbar
  • Page title auto-filled in Harvest
  • Track writing, planning, and project work without leaving Notion
