Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Snap for Harvest.

General

Is Snap for Harvest free?

Yes! Snap for Harvest is completely free forever. You just need an active Harvest subscription to track time.

Do I need a Harvest account?

Yes, you need an active Harvest account. If you don't have one, sign up for Harvest.

Which browsers are supported?

Chrome 88+ and all Chromium-based browsers (Edge, Brave, Opera, etc.).

Privacy & Security

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your Harvest credentials stay in your browser and are never sent to our servers. The extension communicates directly with Harvest's API. We don't collect, store, or sell any of your data.

What permissions does the extension need?

The extension needs permission to access tabs and storage to function, and access to specific sites you enable for time tracking.

Features

Can I disable specific integrations?

Yes! Go to Options → Default Sites and toggle off any integration you don't want to use.

Does this work offline?

No, you need an internet connection to sync with Harvest.

Can I track time on multiple sites at once?

You can have the timer open on multiple sites, but Harvest only allows one active timer at a time.
