Notion Integration

Track time on Notion pages with the page title automatically captured in your Harvest timer.

How to Use

  1. Open any Notion page
  2. Look for the Snap for Harvest button in the toolbar (next to the Share button)
  3. Click the Snap for Harvest button to open the timer
  4. The current page title will be automatically pre-filled in the Notes field
  5. Select your project and task
  6. Click Start Timer

Features

Automatic Page Title Capture

The extension automatically captures the Notion page title and pre-fills it in your Harvest timer. No manual entry needed!

Works Everywhere in Notion

  • Regular pages: Documents, notes, wikis
  • Databases: Tables, boards, calendars, galleries
  • Peek/Preview mode: When viewing pages in a modal
  • Full-page view: When pages are open in full screen

Dynamic Context Updates

When you navigate to a different Notion page, the timer updates with the new page title automatically.

Tips & Best Practices

Button Location

The Snap for Harvest button appears in the Notion toolbar, right next to the Share button. It's always visible regardless of which page you're on.

Enable/Disable Integration

To toggle the Notion integration:

  1. Click the extension icon → Options
  2. Go to Default Sites tab
  3. Toggle Notion on or off

Common Questions

Why isn't the button appearing?

Make sure the Notion integration is enabled in Options → Default Sites, and refresh your Notion page.

Does this work with Notion databases?

Yes! The button works on all Notion content types including databases, wikis, and regular pages.

Can I track time on shared Notion pages?

Yes, as long as you have access to view the page, you can track time on it.
